SafeFall: Fall Detection App

A fall detection system that monitors webcam or mobile phone video streams, identifies fall events using a custom-trained YOLOv8 model, and alerts caregivers with timestamped logs.

The Challenge

Falls are a leading cause of injury, especially among the elderly. Real-time detection and caregiver alerting is often absent or dependent on expensive wearable hardware.

The Solution

Developed as a semi-finalist submission for the Samsung Innovation Campus (Batch 5), this software-based approach utilizes computer vision to monitor environments continuously.

Features

  • Supports both webcam and mobile phone stream input
  • Logs every detection with a timestamp and confidence score
  • Displays caregiver contact information for rapid notification

Tech Stack

Python, OpenCV, YOLOv8 (Ultralytics), Pandas, Streamlit
